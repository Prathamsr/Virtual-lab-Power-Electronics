Three Phase Half Wave Silicon Controlled Rectifier (SCR)

Pre Test

Q1. In a three-phase half wave rectifier usually, why is the primary side of the transformer delta connected?

a. it has no neutral connection

b. we can get greater output voltage

c. it provides a path for the triplen harmonics

d. it provides better temperature stability

Q2. The power delivery and efficiency is low in which rectifier in reference to phases?

a. Single

b. Three

c. Both a and b

d. None of these

Q3. Which of the following is the expression for the average output voltage in three phase half wave controlled rectifier with RL load?

a. `(3)/(2π) V_(mp) cosα`

b. `(3√3)/(2π) V_(mp) cosα`

c. `(3√3)/(π) V_(mp) cosα`

d. `(3√3)/(2π) V_(ml) cosα`

Q4. In which one of the following rectifiers is the direction of power flow is the source to load only?

a. Controlled

b. Uncontrolled

c. Both a and b

d. None of these

Q5. For the three-phase half-wave controlled rectifier circuit with load resistance of 40Ω the input supply is balanced three-phase with line-to-line voltage rating of 400 V, 50 Hz. Find the average output voltage (`V_(out)`) for a firing angle α = 60°?

a. 155 V

b. 200 V

c. 135 V

d. 250 V
